About Us

We are a group of enthusiastic Engineering students majoring in Computer and Communication Engineering (CCE) and Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), at the American University of Beirut. We have various interests in coding, data science, and web development as well as in product management and consulting. As students in the university, we have noticed the amount of hardship most of our mates are going through on a daily basis when trying to reserve transportation back home due to the lack of safe public means, affordable fuel for the car, and the continuously occurring traffic. This has consequently urged us to utilize our expertise in developing a website to facilitate carpooling among the university's students with a high level of safety and professionalism. And here we present you our website "AUBCoRide" which will take your college life at the American University of Beirut to another level.
