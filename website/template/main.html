Free and Safe

Our team of enthusiastic students recognized the daily transportation challenges faced by fellow students, such as the lack of safe public transportation options, rising fuel costs, and persistent traffic congestion.

Made by Students for Students

"AUBCoRide" is a collaborative project by a group of dedicated Engineering students majoring in Computer and Communication Engineering (CCE) and Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with a minor in Business at the American University of Beirut.

Driven by Passion and Innovation.

Driven by our passion for technology and innovation, we decided to harness our skills to address this issue by developing a cutting-edge carpooling website.

AUBCoRide's Mission.

This project represents our commitment to enhancing the college experience for our peers, fostering a sense of community, and contributing to sustainability efforts on campus.

More Than Just a Website.

"AUBCoRide" is more than just a website; it's a solution to transportation challenges that will bring students together and simplify their daily commutes.
